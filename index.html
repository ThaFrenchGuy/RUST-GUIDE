3 rules that win wipes

  1. Bank often: recycle in short bursts; depot scrap/comp constantly.
  2. Upgrade smart: doors → key walls → roof control → externals.
  3. Fight on purpose: no cover / no exit / no reason = don’t fight.

First 30 minutes checklist

  • Tools + bag + bow/crossy path
  • 2 stashes (insurance)
  • 2x1 + airlock (fast shelter beats perfect spot)
  • Furnace down ASAP
  • Pick a recycler loop and stick to it

Bases

Solo/Duo priorities that actually survive.

Starter rules

  • Airlock first.
  • TC not visible from the main door.
  • Door count matters: add doors before expanding size.
  • Roof access is mobility and defense.

Farming

Cloth + teas = quiet power.

Hemp first

  • Cloth = meds, bags, armor.
  • Keep farms hidden. Farms attract raids.

Teas that matter

  • Ore tea: faster node farming.
  • Wood tea: faster expansion.
  • Scrap tea: better monument runs.

PvP

Positioning > aim. Aim still matters.

10 rules

  1. Cover first. Shooting second.
  2. Never loot in the open.
  3. Always have an exit route.
  4. Third-party > fair fights.
  5. Reposition after damage.
  6. Don’t chase into unknown terrain.
  7. Heal behind cover only.
  8. Don’t roam stacked when rich.
  9. Sound is info. Use it.
  10. Leave when the objective is done.

Raiding

Cost mindset and timing.

Door vs wall

  • Raiders pick the cheapest path. Don’t show it.
  • Door paths are cheap defense early.
  • Extern TCs later = grief prevention + higher cost.

Defense basics

  • Traps first, turrets later (unless you’re rich).
  • Turrets slow pushes; they don’t win alone.
  • Spread loot. Avoid jackpot rooms.
